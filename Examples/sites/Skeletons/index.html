Types of Skeletons

(An animated element)

  • Basic Skeleton

    The go-to skeleton for decorations, basic symbols, and classrooms.

  • Real Skeleton

    The select skeleton for graveyards, collectors, and the inside of your body.

  • Evil Skeleton

    The only skeleton you should avoid and fear. It is dangerous.

  • Skeletons in the Closet

    Despite being a metaphorical skeleton, this spooky calcification is often thought to be
    the most fearful of all.
